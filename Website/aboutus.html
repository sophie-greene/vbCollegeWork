About Us

Techbeck Cyber Café is owned by Starbecks Tea and Coffee Emporium. We are dedicated to providing locals and tourists alike with access to the Internet, where they may explore the wonders of the World Wide Web and make use of e-mail facilities.


We provide friendly help and support for novices and experienced alike in a relaxed environment. Other services are available including instant messaging applications (MSN, ICQ, Yahoo!).
