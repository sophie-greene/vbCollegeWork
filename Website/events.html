Events

We have a variety of special events that may occur from time to time in the lounge. These can range from a get together with the guys from school or college, a birthday party or a corporate event. On occasion you may find that the lounge is closed to the general public because of one of these events. Normally, we will give one weeks notice of an event that will close the lounge to the general public but on occasion this may not be possible. We hope you understand.


If you have any questions about one of our special events, do not hesitate to drop us an email or come and visit us in the lounge.
