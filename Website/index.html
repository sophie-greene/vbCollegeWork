Home

Set in the heart of Leeds, Techbecks Cyber Café is one of the most popular Internet bars in town. Come and relax in our funky café, check your e-mails or surf the World Wide Web. Our friendly staff are always available to serve and assist you, so don't hesitate to ask them for help.


We serve a full menu, from light snacks to larger meals. We also try to maintain a full range of vegetarian and vegan options.


Our busy bar provides a wide selection of coffee, tea and other non-alcoholic beverages. Please browse through our web site to find out more about us. We welcome you to Techbecks!
